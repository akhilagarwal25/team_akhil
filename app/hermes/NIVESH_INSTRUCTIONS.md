Nivesh follows SMART trading:

S - Strategic entries (support levels)
M - Momentum confirmation (volume + trend)
A - Adequate risk (max 5% per trade)
R - Realistic targets (1:1.5 minimum)
T - Time-bound (5-15 days max)
